Welcome

“The beauty of chess is it can be whatever you want it to be. It transcends language, age, race, religion, politics, gender, and socioeconomic background. Whatever your circumstances, anyone can enjoy a good fight to the death over the chess board.”

-Simon Williams

UPenn Chess Club invites you to our community! Here you will find all kinds of people, ranging from the quantitative trader to the dedicated volunteer at West Philly. Ultimately, we all love this fun and intellectual game, and we wish to share it with the rest of the community.

Whether you have played chess your whole life or have no idea how the pieces move, we encourage you to come to our meetings and events. Anyone is welcome, even non-Penn students.

Updates:

2021-22: We are back in person and ready to begin hosting in-person events once again! We will have weekly meetings every Wednesday at Houston Hall at 8pm. Keep up with our emails and Facebook group to stay updated!

Check back for updates! We've got big things in store.
